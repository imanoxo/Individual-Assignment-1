Why Public Relations?

Public Relations (PR) is the art of communication and it's about shaping how people see a brand, a company, or a person. I am passionate about helping brands connect with the world through clear, honest, and compelling storytelling.

Steps to Achieve My PR Dream

  1. Obtain a degree in New Media Communication.
  2. Actively participate in campus media or PR clubs.
  3. Complete internships at PR or marketing agencies.
  4. Build a strong online portfolio and personal brand.
  5. Attend workshops and PR-related conferences.
  6. Gain experience as PR assistant → Executive → Strategist.
